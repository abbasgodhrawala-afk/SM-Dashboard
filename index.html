Responsible AI Governance
Principles adopted 2018 · Updated 2023
Fairness
AI systems treat all people fairly, avoiding unequal treatment based on age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, or race.
Reliability & Safety
AI systems perform reliably and safely across expected and unexpected conditions, with robust failure modes.
Privacy & Security
AI respects privacy and maintains data security; systems encourage privacy-preserving techniques.
Inclusiveness
AI should empower everyone and engage people. Benefits should be broadly accessible across societies.
Transparency
AI systems should be understandable. Designers describe purpose, methods, and limitations plainly.
Accountability
People who design and deploy AI systems must be held accountable. AETHER Committee oversees AI ethics.
